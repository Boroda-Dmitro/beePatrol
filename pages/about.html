About Us

Our History

beePatrol, a company based on Maui, is dedicated to the conservation of bee populations and the promotion of honey, as well as the protection of bees and the safe relocation of migrating swarms. Ten years ago, a group of nature enthusiasts and bee lovers came together to make the world a better place for these vital insects. Their mission was to preserve bee populations and raise awareness of the importance of natural honey. After years of dedicated work and in-depth research on bee biology, this team established a special company called "beePatrol." It took on the mission of rescuing bees and spreading awareness about their significance. One of the most significant achievements of beePatrol has been the development of a safe method for relocating migrating bee swarms from places where they may pose risks to people and bees. Instead of destroying swarms, our experts relocate them to safe locations where these insects can continue their crucial work in a natural environment. beePatrol has become a place where people can learn about bees, share their knowledge, and participate in various environmental programs. The company also promotes honey produced in the cleanest conditions and distributes it among local residents, contributing to the growth of bee populations and raising awareness of their importance in our world. Over the last decade, beePatrol, based on Maui, has become a true platform for bee protection and the promotion of environmentally conscious living, and this story is far from over.
